VVVVVV is a platform game all about exploring one simple mechanical idea.

What if you FLIPPED GRAVITY instead of jumping?

Help! Everyone has been teleported away randomly! As the heroic Captain Viridian, it's up to you to find your friends, bring them back to safety, and save the universe!

The game is designed to be an open-world environment. In VVVVVV there are no locks, no power-ups, no switches, nothing to stop you from progressing except the challenges themselves.
