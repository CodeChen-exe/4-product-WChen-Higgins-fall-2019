VVVVVV is a platform game all about exploring one simple mechanical idea.

What if you FLIPPED GRAVITY instead of jumping?

Help! Everyone has been teleported away randomly! As the heroic Captain Viridian, it's up to you to find your friends, bring them back to safety, and save the universe!

The game is designed to be an open-world environment. In VVVVVV there are no locks, no power-ups, no switches, nothing to stop you from progressing except the challenges themselves.

Captain Viridian

As the main protagonist of VVVVVV, Viridian is the Captain of the D.S.S. Souleye and the leader of the six.

Doctor Violet

The 'group mom.' She is willing to point our brave captain viridian in the right direction if he ever gets lost on his quest

Professor Vitellary

A real wise guy, with an unhealthy tendency to break the fourth wall.

Officer Vermilion

There is not a scaredy bone in the courageous officer's body. He's bold and reackless, which gives him all the charateristics of the perfect friend

Chief Verdigris

Verdigris here is in charge of the ship's repairs. His only short coming is that he was born without the ability to flip (gravity).

Doctor Victoria

Despite being scared of... many things, Victoria is a faithful member of the team. She'd rather be in her lab than out socializing.

The editor is one of the many gamemodes in VVVVVV. It allows the user (you!) to make levels and puzzles with a half-complex set of tools.
